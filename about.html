OUR STORY

From a Village Dream to a National Mission

VIGRID was founded with a simple but powerful dream – to solve the power problems in villages with smart, sustainable and affordable solutions.

Our founder, Vivek Kumar, grew up seeing the challenges rural families face – no electricity, no light for studies, limited opportunities.

With 8+ years of experience in electronics and a passion for innovation, he built VIGRID – a brand that delivers complete solar energy solutions in one box.

WHY WE EXIST

Because Every Home Deserves Light

Millions of rural homes still live without reliable electricity. Children study in the dark, families struggle with daily tasks.

We believe clean energy should be a right, not a privilege. VIGRID is here to bridge that gap – one home at a time.
